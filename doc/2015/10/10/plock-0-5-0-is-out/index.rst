Plock 0-5-0 is out
==================

.. post:: 2015/10/10
    :category: Plone

**Pip installs Plock. Plock installs Plone.**

The Plock installer for Plone has been **updated to support Plone 5**. To try Plone 5, just cut & paste::

    pip install plock
    mkdir plone
    plock plone
    plone/bin/plone fg

Then open `localhost <http://localhost:8080>`_:
